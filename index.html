What is it?

E-CoGar is a vertical garden which also has a compost at the bottom. To mantain everything ecological we included a automatic irrigation system powered by solar panels.

We provide you the solar panels, wood, baskets and nails, to assamble the mold for the garden and some seeds to plant.

This is a easy way to help the Earth each day, by doing a very simple and easy thing.

A garden, compost, and well-being of earth all at once!

The best way to help the environment.
